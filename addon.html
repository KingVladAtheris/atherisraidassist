In-game Addon

Player Widget

Use /araw to open the personal widget.

The selected raid = current data loaded in the addon
(assignments • tactical maps • soft reserves)

→ Shows your personal assignment for the current boss
→ Big button to quickly open the tactical map
→ Green border around your icon = easy to spot where to stand

Raid Creation and Management

Use /ara to open the main raid management window

You can:
• Create a new raid
• Import from string (most commonly from the Discord bot)
• Add players manually, from current group/raid, or from import

Encounter Management

Create new encounters or load presets from the dropdown.

For each encounter you can:
• Attach a tactical map
• Choose background image
• Place raider markers and symbols to explain mechanics & assignments

Spreadsheet Roster Planning

Full spreadsheet-style interface for fast roster management.

• Slots are numbered and grouped by role
• Click to assign / remove a raider from your roster
• Makes swapping people very fast during raid preparation
• Lets you keep the same assignments and reuse them with different rosters

Soft Reserves, Export & Broadcast

Soft reserves appear directly in item tooltips — no external lists needed.

• Export entire raid to string (Ctrl+A → Ctrl+C)
• Broadcast raid data to everyone in group/raid
 (requires them to have the addon installed)
